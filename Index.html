FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NexaChain and our ecosystem.

What is NexaChain?

NexaChain is a next-generation blockchain platform designed for decentralized finance (DeFi) applications. It offers lightning-fast transactions, minimal fees, and advanced smart contract capabilities, making it ideal for developers and users looking for a scalable and secure blockchain solution.

How do I buy NEXA tokens?

NEXA tokens can be purchased through our official DEX (NexaSwap) or through partnered centralized exchanges. Simply connect your Web3 wallet, select the trading pair, and complete your swap. Always ensure you're using official links to avoid scams.

What wallets are supported?

NexaChain supports all major Web3 wallets including MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, and Ledger hardware wallets. We recommend using a hardware wallet for maximum security when holding significant amounts.

How can I earn passive income with NexaChain?

There are multiple ways to earn with NexaChain: stake your NEXA tokens to earn up to 12.5% APY, provide liquidity on NexaSwap to earn trading fees, or participate in yield farming programs. All rewards are distributed automatically to your wallet.

Is NexaChain audited?

Yes, NexaChain has been thoroughly audited by leading blockchain security firms including CertiK, Hacken, and OpenZeppelin. All audit reports are publicly available on our documentation page. We conduct regular security assessments and bug bounty programs.

How do I become a validator?

To become a validator, you need to stake a minimum of 100,000 NEXA tokens and run a validator node. Detailed instructions are available in our documentation. Validators earn block rewards and a share of transaction fees for securing the network.
